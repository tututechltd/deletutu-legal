Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

The short version: Your photos, videos, and library metadata never leave your device — all photo analysis runs on-device using Apple's PhotoKit framework. We collect only anonymous app-usage analytics and crash reports through Firebase to fix bugs and improve the app. We do not collect your photos, contacts, or location. There are no user accounts. Subscriptions are handled by Apple — we never see your payment details.

1. Who We Are

Deletutu is developed by Tututech Ltd. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at tututech.ltd@gmail.com.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, process, or store:

  • Your photos or videos
  • Photo metadata (locations, faces, timestamps, file names)
  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Your IDFA (advertising identifier) — App Tracking Transparency is not requested
  • Your contacts (used only locally on your device for face search)
  • Your precise or coarse location

3. Information We Do Collect (Analytics & Crash Reporting)

We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics, both from Google, to understand how the app is used and to diagnose crashes. The data sent is aggregate app-usage information and crash reports — never linked to your identity:

  • Usage events — which screens you open, which features you tap, which language you use, and how often you use the app
  • Diagnostic data — app version, OS version, device model, crash logs, and performance metrics
  • Firebase App Instance ID — a per-install random identifier generated by the Firebase SDK that is reset by reinstalling the app. This ID is not linked to your name, Apple ID, email, IDFA, or any other personal identifier.

This data falls under Apple's "Usage Data", "Diagnostics", and "Identifiers" categories on the App Store Privacy Nutrition Label, and is declared as "Not Linked to You" and "Not Used for Tracking". We never send your photos, your contacts, your location, or any free-text input (search queries are sent only as length and has-results booleans — never the actual text).

You can reset the Firebase App Instance ID at any time by deleting and reinstalling the app.

4. Photo Library Access

Deletutu requires access to your device's photo library to function. This access is used exclusively and entirely on your device to:

  • Display your photos and videos for review
  • Analyze photos for blur and duplicate detection (all processing is on-device)
  • Delete photos you choose to remove (via iOS's native Photos system)
  • Play video previews inline

Your photos are read and analyzed locally using Apple's PhotoKit framework. No photo data ever leaves your device.

You can revoke photo library access at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos → Deletutu.

5. Deleted Photos

When you delete a photo using Deletutu, it is moved to the iOS Photos Recycle Bin (Recently Deleted album). Photos remain there for 30 days and can be recovered before permanent deletion. This behavior is controlled entirely by iOS — Deletutu does not bypass it.

6. Subscriptions

Deletutu offers Premium access via auto-renewable subscriptions:

  • Yearly Premium — $29.99 / year, with a 3-day free trial for new subscribers
  • Weekly Premium — $4.99 / week

All payments are processed by Apple via your Apple ID. Tututech Ltd never receives, stores, or has access to your payment information, credit card details, billing address, or Apple ID. Apple sends us only an anonymous, aggregated count of subscribers per region so that we can understand demand — never linked to any individual user.

Subscriptions auto-renew at the end of each period unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage and cancel your subscription anytime in Settings → [Your Apple ID] → Subscriptions on your device, or via the in-app "Manage Subscription" button.

The 3-day free trial converts to a paid subscription automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the trial ends. Cancellation during the trial is free — you will not be charged.

7. Third-Party Services

Deletutu uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple — handles subscription billing and the StoreKit purchase flow (section 6).
  • Google Firebase Analytics — receives aggregate app-usage events for product analytics. See Google's Firebase Privacy & Security documentation.
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics — receives crash reports and stack traces so we can diagnose and fix crashes. Same privacy terms as Firebase Analytics.

We do not use advertising SDKs, attribution SDKs, or any cross-app/cross-site tracking. We do not enable the IDFA (App Tracking Transparency is not requested).

8. Children's Privacy

Deletutu is rated 4+ on the App Store and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personally-identifiable information from anyone, including children under 13. The analytics described in section 3 are not linked to identity.

9. Data Security

Photo and video content stays on your device and is protected by iOS's built-in security features. The aggregate analytics described in section 3 are transmitted to Google over TLS and stored in Google's infrastructure under their security and compliance posture (ISO 27001, SOC 2, etc.).

10. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review it periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
