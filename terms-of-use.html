5. Deletion of Your Photos — Important

When you swipe a photo for deletion in Deletutu, the App calls Apple's PhotoKit deletion API. iOS then shows you a system confirmation sheet; only after you confirm there does iOS move the photo to its "Recently Deleted" album. iOS retains the photo in that album for 30 days, during which you can restore it from the Photos app. After 30 days iOS permanently removes the photo. Deletutu does not bypass that 30-day grace period and cannot recover photos once iOS has permanently deleted them.

You are responsible for maintaining your own backups. We strongly recommend using iCloud Photos, an external backup, or both before running a large cleanup session.
